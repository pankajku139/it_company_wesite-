About Us

We deal with the aspects of professional IT Services

NIS is a leading provider of technology solutions & IT Consultancy for business and government. We are the official Dell distributor for delivering technology products & services at your door step. Our endeavor is to provide one stop solution for all computer related purchase.
NIS features dedicated account managers who help customers choose the right technology products and services to best meet their unique needs.
